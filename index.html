is a goodbye ever forever ?


After suffering from a loss, the player sees himself entering a new world where he has to face an emotional journey through the different stages of grief.

He is not alone.

As the game progresses, a journey helper enters the scene with a soul-like appearance and, in the end revealed to be connected to the character.




Get in touch with our universe besides the game itself!

Visit the different pages and interact with environments and characters. In addition to our other ways to reach out to our users, we developed this interactive gallery. This gives another focus on the models we designed and gives you freedom to explore the game without affecting your gameplay experience.

You can follow us on social media to stay updated on the game's development and other releases. Or, to get closer to the gameplay, access our prototypes and test them!
